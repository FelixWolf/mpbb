Abusive seller report form

False, spam, or troll reports may result in being banned from this service.
Sellers may be notified of abusive false reports to help protect them.

Help with reporting

  • Contact info
    We use this to contact you if we need more information. This information will not be made public with exception of username, which will be displayed as initials in the report.
  • Collecting evidence
    Please provide screenshots of the original asset(EG: Model comparisons, screenshots from a game, etc). Here is an example of good evidence. These can be uploaded to Imgur or other image sharing sites.
  • First name and Last name
    This is not your or their real life information! This is your and their Second Life username. If their name is like "Chaser Zaks", you put "Chaser" as first name, "Zaks" as last name. If it looks like "Chasertester", you put "Chasertester" as first name and "Resident" as last name.
  • Comments
    If you have information you want to put out about the seller, such as hostile interactions, false review flagging, etc. Please feel free to bring it up here.
  • Consent and Liability
    This is to ensure people do not file false claims. Consenting to contact is not required to submit a report, but lets us know if you would be willing to help follow up evidence reports.

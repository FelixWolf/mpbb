Reporting Abuse

Yes and no.
We use your avatar's initials. EG, if you have the username Chaser Zaks, you will be shown as "C.Z".
I am aware this will result in name conflicts. This is intentional.
Firstly, thank you very much for your report! It is very valuable. However because people will abuse this, I do moderate each and every report to make sure that it is genuine.
Your report will still be visible on the site, but it will be in a PENDING state, as such has no effect on the store's status. Once I confirm the evidence is legit and isn't spam, it will be CONFIRMED.
No worries! As long as the report is in the PENDING state, it will be editable. You can remove and add information as much as you need to.

Filing an Appeal

1. Remove all the ripped / stolen / illegal acquired content from your store.
2. Go to https://mpbb.softhyena.com/appeal.
3. Fill out required information.
4. Submit and wait.

Appeals will be made PUBLICLY VISIBLE, minus email address and contact details. So please avoid putting personally identifying information in any other field that isn't for contact purposes.
Repeated false or non-complying appeals will result in banning from the appeal system and may result in permanent listing on the site.
I manually go through each and every appeal. You will need to give me at least a week to process them.
Comply with the requirements in the appeal rejection notice and file another appeal.
Repeated false or non-complying appeals will result in banning from the appeal system and may result in permanent listing on the site.
Please file a support ticket at https://support.softhyena.com/ or contact Chaser Zaks in-world.
